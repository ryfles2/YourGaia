Biological Park

it is the permanent centre of environmental education installed by the Municipal Council of Vila Nova de Gaia, in 1983, in the valley of the river Febros and managed by the Municipal Company Biological Park of Gaia. The main objective is to preserve the typical landscape of the region, while it was essentially a large agricultural area. The park has a modern Visitor Reception Centre, with auditorium, hostel, self-service, documentation centre, laboratory and permanent and temporary exhibition halls.

Municipal swimming pool of Granja

The outdoor pool assimilates a ludic component, only attending in times of greater heat, since it is at room temperature. Its location by the sea allows the use of salt water, after being properly treated, for therapeutic purposes such as thalassotherapy.
